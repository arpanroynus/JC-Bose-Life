Stage 2: Class 8
Regur & Self-Plowing

In Class 8, we call this Regur Soil. It's found in the Deccan Trap. Its most famous feature is that it develops deep cracks during the dry season.

This is actually a good thing! The cracks allow air to reach deep into the ground. When it rains, the soil swells up again, "plowing" itself naturally without any help from a tractor.

This soil has serious trust issues; it literally "cracks up" the moment it stops raining.
