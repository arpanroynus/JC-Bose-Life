Stage 2: Class 8
Regur Soil

In the Deccan Trap, this is called Regur Soil. It develops deep cracks in summer which helps it "self-plow" by letting oxygen deep into the ground.

This soil has trust issues; it literally "cracks up" when it stops raining.

Chemical Composition

Class 10 exams require the term Titaniferous Magnetite for its black color. It is highly argillaceous (clayey) and perfect for the Cotton Belt.

Exam Tip: Write "Titaniferous Magnetite" or the examiner will think your prep is as thin as desert soil!
